Privacy Policy

Dentalux Dental Clinic — Batumi, Georgia
Last updated: 7 May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Dentalux LLC ("Dentalux", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with the Dentalux Instagram messaging assistant ("the Service"). By sending a message to @dentalux.ge on Instagram, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

When you message @dentalux.ge on Instagram, we receive the following information through Meta's Instagram Messaging API:

  • Your Instagram-scoped user ID (a unique identifier provided by Instagram)
  • The message content you send
  • Timestamp of the message
  • Public profile information made available by Instagram (username, profile picture)

We do not collect: passwords, financial data, contact lists, location, or any data outside the messages you send to us.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Reply to your inquiries about dental services, pricing, appointments, and clinic information
  • Provide automated multilingual responses (Georgian, English, Russian) using AI
  • Maintain a short-term conversation history (kept for the duration of the active conversation only) for context

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses. Message content may be transmitted to Anthropic's API for the sole purpose of generating a reply. See Anthropic's privacy policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with:

  • Meta / Instagram — the platform delivering your messages to us
  • Anthropic — the AI provider generating responses
  • Railway — our hosting provider, which processes data in transit only

We may disclose information if required by law (e.g., a valid legal request from Georgian authorities).

5. Data Retention

Conversation history is held in volatile memory only and is automatically discarded when the conversation becomes inactive or when the server restarts. We do not maintain a persistent database of your messages.

6. Your Rights

You may, at any time:

  • Stop interacting with the Service by simply not sending further messages
  • Block @dentalux.ge on Instagram to permanently cease communication
  • Request deletion of any retained information by emailing dentalux.costumers@gmail.com
  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for individuals 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents of minor patients should book appointments via phone rather than the messaging assistant.

8. Security

Communication between Instagram, our servers, and Anthropic is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. We maintain reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this URL with the "Last updated" date at the top.
